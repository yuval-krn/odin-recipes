Jessie's Brekkie

Description

My girlfriend and I enjoyed this simple, (relatively) healthy breakfast all throughout our summer. We were both working full time so it provided a quick, easy, and approachable outlet to appease our hunger.

Feel free to swap out the breakfast sausage with any other protein of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • Butter
  • Breakfast Sausage
  • Avocado
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Sea salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook breakfast sausage in a heated pan, following package instructions.
  2. Remove sausage into plate, freeing pan up for the eggs.
  3. Add butter into the pan until the ancestors tell you to stop.
  4. Crack eggs directly into pan and mix, hoping for the best.
  5. While eggs cook, slice up your avocado and toast your bread.
  6. Once eggs have solidified, throw sausage briefly back in to heat up.
  7. Hit everything with salt and pepper, hot sauce for the boyfriend.
  8. Plate and enjoy :)
